I recently earned my amateur-radio license and have been learning through a mixture of operating, antenna building, testing, and experimentation.

This site will be my record of that work.

## Current equipment

- AnyTone AT-D878UVII Plus
- Baofeng BF-F8HP
- NanoVNA
- Signal Stuff antenna
- Half-built, homemade yagi antenna
- (Bingfu dual band antenna with mag mount)[https://a.co/d/03S0yPUY]

## Current projects

I’m currently working on antennas for the 2-meter and 70-centimeter bands, including a ground-plane antenna and a Yagi that is intended to hopefully one day working the ISS!

I’ll use this site to document the designs, mistakes, measurements, and results.
